About Us

Based in Srisailam, Andhra Pradesh, Herbst offers a refreshing range of beverages including traditional Goli Sodas, flavour-packed Sabja seed drinks, and pure alkaline water. We're committed to expanding across districts within the state.

We proudly use glass bottles only for all beverages (except 5L and 10L water cans, which are in plastic), and we have all the required FSSAI quality clearances.

Herbst is also a women-first company — the majority of our workforce are women.

Our Products

  • Goli Soda Flavours: Orange, Lemon, Nimbu Pani, Jeera, Jaljeera, Strawberry, Sugandhi
  • Sabja Seed Drinks: Multiple flavours coming soon
  • Water: 500ml, 1L, 5L, and 10L – branded as "Nallamalla Hill Alkaline Water"
  • Flavoured Milk (Coming Soon): Badam, Pista, Strawberry, Chocolate

Water Purification Process

Our alkaline water goes through:

  • Sand Filtration
  • Activated Carbon Filtration
  • UV Treatment
  • Reverse Osmosis
  • Ozonation
