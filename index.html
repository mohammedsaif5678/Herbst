About Us

At Herbst, we’re committed to delivering high-quality beverages made with care — from our refreshing sodas to our health-boosting sabja drinks. Headquartered in Srisailam, Andhra Pradesh, we’re proudly expanding to bring our taste to more communities.

Our People: Empowering Women in the Workplace

At Herbst, we proudly employ a majority of women in our workforce. We believe in empowering women, fostering equality, and building a strong, diverse team. Together, we work to create beverages that are as refreshing as the values we stand for.

Our Packaging: Quality in Every Drop

While we take pride in using glass bottles for our consumer-sized drinks to ensure freshness and sustainability, we also offer 5-litre and 10-litre cans exclusively for bottled water. These larger containers, made with high-quality plastic, are designed to preserve the purity and freshness of our water for bulk customers and businesses.

Sabja Seed Drinks

Sabja seed drinks are a refreshing and healthful choice, packed with fiber, antioxidants, and essential minerals. They aid in digestion, regulate body temperature, and offer a natural energy boost, making them perfect for a healthy lifestyle.

Bottled Water (Nallamalla Hill Alkaline Water)

Pure, refreshing, and full of nature’s goodness — Nallamalla Hill Alkaline Water is sourced from the pristine Nallamalla Hills. It undergoes a multi-step purification process, including sand filtration, activated carbon filtration, UV treatment, reverse osmosis, and ozonation to ensure the highest quality and purity. Drink with confidence and experience hydration at its finest.
